U.K

The United Kingdom (U.K.) is a country in northwestern Europe made up of England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland. Its capital is London, a major global city. The U.K. has a constitutional monarchy with a parliamentary democracy. It’s known for its rich history (from the British Empire to the Industrial Revolution), diverse culture, world-class universities, and global influence in politics, finance, science, and the arts.

    U.K. Activities
  • Visit Big Ben and the Houses of Parliament in London
  • Explore Edinburgh Castle in Scotland
  • Go hiking in the Lake District

Paris

Paris is the capital city of France, known as the “City of Light.” It is famous for landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral. Paris is also a global center for fashion, art, culture, and history. The River Seine flows through the city, adding to its charm.

    Paris Activities
  • See the Eiffel Tower and enjoy the view
  • Visit the Louvre Museum (Mona Lisa!)
  • Walk along the Seine River or Champs-Élysées
